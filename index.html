Teaching & Learning Design

Teaching as the foundation of my science communication practice.

From 2013 to 2019, I taught biology throughout my PhD as both a Primary Instructor and Teaching Assistant. Teaching in a new country and academic culture shaped how I communicate science clearly, empathetically, and without assumptions about prior knowledge.
In 2015, I received the Teaching Excellence Award in recognition of clear explanation, inclusive pedagogy, and effective engagement with diverse learners. These skills now directly inform my public-facing science communication work.
