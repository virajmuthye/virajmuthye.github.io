About Me

Community, Connection, and Communication are the core of all of my endeavours. Science becomes meaningful when it is shared, questioned, and experienced together.

My work focuses on building spaces where people can encounter science with curiosity rather than intimidation.

Teaching & Learning Design

Teaching remains the foundation of my science communication practice. I taught introductory biology at Iowa State University from 2013 to 2019.

Teaching in a new country shaped how I communicate science clearly, empathetically, and without assumptions about prior knowledge.

Creative Science Alliance

I founded the Creative Science Alliance to design hands-on, Calgary-based STEAM outreach rooted in local ecology.

Arts and science are two sides of the same coin and neither can be experienced in isolation.
